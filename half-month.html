Half-Month

June 30, 2026

June, June, Juny, Juny, Juny, Juny! *bonus points for singing the correct tune in your head*

Sticking true to not forcing the writing while not sticking as true to uploading said writing on time.

Half of June was spent galavanting across the U.S.: LA, to Charlottesville, to Dallas, to Houston. There were too many euphoric moments to recount them all but here are the top 10 (in no particular order):

1. Getting stopped by TSA in LAX in order for them to inspect my Ggiata sandwich

2. Spending the morning in Charlottesville talking to my old soccer coach about what’s new in the college soccer world and walking around Grounds

3. Friday welcome party to Virg table-top pipeline

4. Argentina vs. Austria World Cup Game with one of my best friends of 16+ yrs (met playing club soccer)

5. Messi scoring the most goals in men's FIFA World Cup history

6. Watching Your Friends & Neighbors each night with my parents

7. Going on a morning walk with my 16yr old cousin (feeling…old?)

8. Crying on the couch with my best friend in the whole entire world (cathartic not concerning)

9. Eating homemade carbonara and chocolate fudge sauce that Cooper’s grandmother spent 3 days preparing (unreal)

10. Trying Tiny’s in Memorial for the first time (chocolate chip cookie+strawberry ice cream is a beautiful combo)

June was a needed slowdown metaphorically without slowing down physically. A perfect balance.
